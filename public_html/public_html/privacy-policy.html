Privacy-Policy

Welcome to BODHIFY ONLINE EDUCATION (OPC) PVT. LTD. ("BODH", "we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

When you access or use our platform, we may collect the following types of information:

1.1 Personal Information

  • Name, date of birth, city, contact details (email, phone number)
  • Guardian details (for ASC students)
  • Education background and skill interests
  • Any other details submitted via forms for enrollment or queries

1.2 Payment Information

  • Payment transactions are securely processed through third-party payment gateways. We do not store credit/debit card details.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address, browser type, device information
  • Cookies and tracking technologies (used for improving user experience)

1.4 Content You Provide

  • Blogs, event participation, feedback, and other submissions to BODH
  • Communications through our platform (queries, reviews, complaints, or support requests)

2.How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:
  • Providing Services: Enrolling students, matching with instructors, scheduling classes, issuing certifications.
  • Payment Processing: Managing payments for academic and skill learning programs.
  • Personalization & Improvement: Customizing learning experiences, improving platform functionality, and resolving technical issues.
  • Security & Compliance: Protecting against unauthorized access, ensuring compliance with legal regulations.
  • Marketing & Communication: Sending relevant updates, newsletters, promotions, and responding to inquiries.
  • Event & Blog Management: Publishing submitted blogs, sharing event details, and showcasing participation.

3.Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share it in the following cases:
  • With Instructors & Coordinators: To ensure smooth learning experiences.
  • With Third-Party Service Providers: For secure payment processing and platform optimization.
  • For Legal Reasons: If required by law or to protect our platform’s security.

4. Privacy for Blogs & Events

4.1 Blogs

  • Content submitted for publication on BODH’s platform remains the intellectual property of the author but may be used by BODH for promotional purposes.
  • By submitting a blog, the author grants BODH the right to edit, modify, and publish the content.
  • Once published, a blog cannot be removed unless a valid request is submitted.

4.2 Events & Photos

  • We may capture and share event photos/videos on our platform, social media, and marketing materials.
  • Participants consent to photography and video recording by attending our events.
  • If you wish to have your image removed, you can submit a request to our support team.

5. Data Protection & Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Secure servers and encryption for sensitive information.
  • Restricted access to personal data, only for authorized personnel.
  • Regular monitoring and updating of security protocols.

Despite these measures, no online platform can guarantee 100% security. Users are responsible for keeping their login credentials confidential.
