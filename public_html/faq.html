Frequently Asked Questions

Can I learn a new skill that isn't listed on the website?

Yes, you can learn any skill even if it's not listed on our website. Simply select "Add a New Skill" in the skill section and provide your requirements. You can also customize the learning content for any skill.

Can I pay the fee in installments?

Yes, you can pay in installments. The installment structure depends on the Total number of sessions and total fee. Please note, if the fee or duration is too Less, installment options may not be available.

Do I get certified?

Yes, you will receive a certificate after completing all the learning sessions and projects for the selected skills. Please note that if you cancel at any point, the certificate will not be issued, and projects will not be included.

How to join as an instructor?

To join as an instructor, please email your resume or portfolio to bodh.contact@gmail.com . Kindly mention the role you're applying for in the subject line. We will then guide you through the next steps.

Do I have language preferences?

Yes, you can learn in the language you are comfortable with. Please let us know your preferred language, and we’ll do our best to provide it.

How can I choose my required skill?

If you need help deciding what to learn, our team is here to guide you based on your interests, with inputs from professionals. Feel free to contact us for any queries.
